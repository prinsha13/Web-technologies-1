BTS in The SEOM

BTS Island: In the SEOM (BTS Island: 인더섬) is a mobile BTS game released on June 28, 2022. This game was created by the kpop artist BTS. It is a story-rich puzzle and a casual, character management game with BTS contributing to its creation. The trailer first appeared during the first day of PERMISSION TO DANCE ON STAGE in Las Vegas.This game was created to be played by anyone and everyone as it is very to easy to understand and play. A game loved by all children from child to adults. It is one of the best puzzle game out there.
